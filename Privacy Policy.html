Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1/29/2026

Data Collection

Pet Nutrition Tracker stores all data locally on your device using SwiftData. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information.

Sign in with Apple

We use Sign in with Apple for authentication. Your Apple ID is used solely for account management and is not shared with third parties.

AI Features

When you use AI features, your queries are sent to Groq and Google Gemini APIs to generate responses. These services have their own privacy policies.

Data Storage

All pet data, meals, medications, and activities are stored locally on your device and are not backed up to our servers.
